Clover Field | About

Clover Field is an interactive website centered on the idea of emulating a clover field. Users must drag and drop clovers around in seek of the elusive four-leaf clovers, with there being five in total on the canvas. Once a four-leaf clover is found, the user will then be asked to input their wish in hopes of it coming true.

Clover Field is intended to be a calming experience whereupon users can relax and enjoy a mundane game of sorting and finding while accompanied by soft music. For their efforts in playing, the users are rewarded with a sense of hope that their wishes will be fulfilled by the found four-leaf clover. In terms of the technical, the website is created using a combination of Createjs's Easeljs and Soundjs. Whereas Easeljs is used for its drag-and-drop function, Soundjs is used for the sound effect the clovers make when they are clicked on.
